Dear conscious human,

Since you enrolled in this introductory course, you must be craving to become more of a conscious coliver, start your coliving journey or maybe even set up your own intentional community!

### This course is for you if you want understand how you can:

- Deepen your conscious connection to yourself, others, and the world around you
- Interact with like-minded people sharing similar values
- Find a sense of belonging
- Increase your support and security
- Increase your health and wellbeing
- Operate at a different pace of life
- Live more intentionally

### \
You will:

- Learn how conscious coliving can be the solution to some of today’s biggest problems
- Understand what it means to live consciously vs unconsciously
- Gain new insight into the benefits of consciously coliving
- Gain awareness into the challenges of consciously coliving
- Know the next steps and how to find conscious coliving situations suited to you

## Over the next 6 lessons, we are going to give you a whole new insight into conscious coliving; its potential and its reality.

After you complete this introductory course, you will never be able to live unconsciously again, you’ll know too much!

We are going to remove you from the isolated unconscious way of living that penetrates so much of our society and gently land you into the world of conscious coliving. A world of greater abundance of time, money and energy. And we’re not just talking about personal gain but social, cultural, wellbeing and environmental gain too!

**Here’s what we’re going to cover:**

#### \
🌎 **Day \#1: Out with the old and in with the new - our way of life is changing**

We’re going to set the scene of where we’re at and how we got here. And, more importantly, we’re going to show you the opportunities now available because of the changing world. By the end of this lesson you’ll be excited to know more!

#### 🏡 **Day \#2: Conscious coliving as a modern method of living and 8 coliving myths busted **

Today we fully dive into explaining what conscious coliving is. We also bust the top myths about coliving that can often get in the way of people fully embracing it. By the end of today’s lesson you will have peace of mind about what conscious coliving actually is.

#### 🏆 **Day \#3: 9 benefits of conscious coliving and 4 challenges to overcome (and why overcoming them might be benefit \#10)**

In this lesson, we introduce you to our protagonist Sam as they explore the benefits and challenges in more depth, providing you with a balanced perspective to really understand if coliving is for you.

#### 🚩 **Day \#4: How to spot a good conscious coliving opportunity and 7 red flags to avoid **

To help you out today we’ve created a checklist of what to look for when considering your coliving options. We’ve also identified the top red flags to be aware of and how to spot these! By the end of today’s lesson you will be fully prepared to explore what an aligned conscious coliving opportunity is for you.

#### 📜 **Day \#5: The 10 commandments for conscious coliving** 

Do you want to know the exact rules you should adhere to so that you don’t become the red flag? Well today, we won’t quite tell you that but we will (with our tongue-in-cheek) offer you some insight into what we recommend you take into consideration to be a conscious coliver.

#### 🎉 **Day \#6: Celebration! And what to do next** 

In the last lesson of the course we help you figure out what to do next with all of the information you’ve received. We even extend an invite to you to join us at one of our amazing residencies to really experience what true conscious coliving is all about. We’re excited to tell you more.
