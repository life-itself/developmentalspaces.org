      <h2 class="ds-display" style="font-size:clamp(1.8rem,3.2vw,2.4rem);margin:0">Initiatives lack capital. Investors lack initiatives.</h2>
      <p style="margin-top:1.25rem">Community-scale properties need substantial capital; groups pool money slowly and borrow badly; and the people best suited to this life are rarely the highest earners. Meanwhile investors who <em>want</em> a stable, ethical home for capital can't find vetted projects. Bridging that gap — bringing confidence to capital, and capital to conscious communities — is what this stream is about.</p>
