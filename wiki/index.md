# Wiki

Working notes on inner development, developmental science, and the design of developmental spaces. This is a **garden, not a book**: notes vary from polished to raw, grow over time, and link to each other. It began as the knowledge base for the *Intro to Deliberately Developmental Spaces* course ([course announcement](https://news.lifeitself.org/p/introduction-to-deliberately-developmental)) and now serves as the wiki layer of Developmental Spaces.

For the polished statements of the ideas, see the [whitepaper](/paper) and the [Build stream](/build).
